# Suno Features Reference

This document lists Suno's current features and capabilities (as of 2026). Use this to answer "is this possible?" questions accurately.

## Plans & Limits

### Free Plan
- 10 songs per day
- Best free model (v4.5-all)
- 4 concurrent generations (shared queue)
- No add-on credits
- Limited commercial rights

### Pro Plan
- 500 songs per month
- Access to advanced models (v4, v4.5, v4.5+, v5, v5.5)
- 10 concurrent generations (priority queue)
- Add-on credits available
- Full commercial rights

### Premier Plan
- 2000 songs per month
- Access to all advanced models
- 10 concurrent generations (priority queue)
- Add-on credits available
- Maximum commercial rights

## Core Creation Features

- **Suno Studio** — Advanced editing and arrangement tools
- **Remix** — Extend, cover, adjust speed, and create variations of existing songs
- **Co-write with Suno** — Collaborative lyric and song building
- **Upload Audio** — Upload your own audio (up to 30 minutes on paid plans)
- **Add Vocals** — Generate or add vocal tracks
- **Add Instrumental** — Generate instrumental versions
- **Inspire** — Get AI suggestions for prompts and ideas

## Model Versions

- v4
- v4.5
- v4.5+
- v5
- v5.5

Higher versions generally offer better quality, coherence, and feature support.

## Key Capabilities

- Generate full songs from text prompts
- Generate songs from custom lyrics
- Create instrumentals
- Extend existing songs
- Create covers
- Adjust song speed/tempo
- Upload and continue from existing audio
- Use custom voices (on higher plans)

## Commercial Use

- Free plan has limited commercial rights
- Pro and Premier plans include full commercial rights (subject to terms)
