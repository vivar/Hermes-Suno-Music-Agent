# Minimax Video Reference

## Overview
Minimax Video is a capable AI video model that emphasizes creative flexibility, good motion dynamics, and artistic output. It works well for stylized music video content and offers a balance between realism and expressive, artistic interpretations.

## Prompt Structure
- Vivid scene setting and subject behavior
- Camera movement and energy level
- Artistic or stylistic direction
- Mood and lighting cues

**Example Prompt:**
"Abstract floating islands connected by glowing bridges. A lone traveler walks across the bridges as the islands slowly rotate. Dreamy, ethereal lighting with soft particle effects. Gentle camera movement following the traveler. Surreal and cinematic atmosphere."

## Best Practices
- Embrace stylized and surreal prompts — Minimax responds well to imaginative concepts
- Use energy descriptors ("energetic", "flowing", "pulsing", "dreamlike")
- Specify color palettes and lighting mood
- Generate shorter clips and composite for longer sequences
- Use image references to anchor characters or key visual elements

## Strengths
- Excellent at stylized, artistic, and surreal visuals
- Good dynamic motion and creative interpretation
- Strong color and atmosphere control
- Works well for abstract or metaphorical music video concepts
- Relatively forgiving with prompt phrasing

## Things to Avoid
- Ultra-realistic human close-ups (can be less consistent than Veo 3 or Kling)
- Overly complex multi-character choreography
- Tiny text or intricate mechanical details

## Advanced Tips
- Great for "lyric visualization" style videos where concepts are metaphorical rather than literal
- Pair with songwriting.md to translate emotional themes into abstract visuals
- Use for atmospheric B-roll and transitional sequences in music videos
- Experiment with different artistic styles (oil painting motion, watercolor, 3D render, etc.)
- Combine with music-production.md for matching visual energy to song sections
