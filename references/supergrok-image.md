# SuperGrok Image Generation Reference

This guide covers best practices for prompting SuperGrok (xAI's image generation model), available through Grok on x.com or the Grok app.

## Overview

SuperGrok delivers high-quality image generation with strong prompt understanding and good realism. It is particularly effective at following detailed instructions and producing clean, well-composed results.

## Prompt Structure Recommendations

SuperGrok responds well to this general flow:

1. **Subject description** (detailed)
2. **Environment and setting**
3. **Action / Pose**
4. **Lighting and mood**
5. **Art style / aesthetic**
6. **Camera and composition**
7. **Quality enhancers**

## Example Prompt Format

```
A mysterious figure standing on a foggy mountain ridge at dawn, wearing a long dark coat, wind blowing through their hair, dramatic golden light breaking through the clouds, cinematic composition, highly detailed, atmospheric, 8k
```

## Best Practices

- Use clear, descriptive natural language prompts
- Be specific about lighting, atmosphere, and mood
- Include details about clothing, textures, and materials
- Mention camera angles and shot types when composition matters
- SuperGrok handles complex scenes reasonably well
- Add quality boosters toward the end of the prompt
- Describe textures and materials relevant to the scene

## Quality Boosters

These commonly improve SuperGrok results:

- highly detailed
- intricate details
- sharp focus
- cinematic lighting
- atmospheric
- photorealistic
- 8k
- ultra realistic
- masterpiece

## Things SuperGrok Excels At

- Realistic human features and expressions
- Good prompt adherence
- Clean compositions
- Natural lighting and shadows
- Atmospheric scenes
- Consistent results with descriptive prompts

## Things to Avoid

- Overly vague or extremely short prompts
- Conflicting style instructions
- Very abstract or surreal concepts without clear visual grounding

## Advanced Tips

- SuperGrok benefits from longer, more detailed prompts
- Lighting references (golden hour, dramatic side lighting, soft diffused light) help significantly
- You can reference film photography styles or specific camera looks for better realism
- Adding "cinematic" or "photorealistic" often improves overall quality
