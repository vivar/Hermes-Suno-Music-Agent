# YouTube SEO Guidelines for HarmoniEH

Condensed best practices for titles, descriptions, tags, and chapters (sourced from HubSpot + Search Engine Journal, May 2026).

## Titles
- Front-load primary keyword in first 50–60 characters.
- Use numbers, power words, and emotional triggers when natural.
- Keep under 70 characters to avoid truncation in search results.
- Avoid clickbait that harms retention.

## Descriptions
- First 100–150 characters are critical (appear in search results).
- Lead with main keyword + hook in the first 1–2 sentences.
- Include timestamps/chapters — YouTube now uses auto-generated chapters as search metadata; manual timestamps override them.
- Add links, CTAs, and hashtags at the bottom.
- Target 200–300+ words for SEO value.

## Tags / Keywords
- Mix of 1–2 broad, 5–8 specific, and 3–5 long-tail tags.
- Always include the exact video title as one tag.
- Prioritize relevance over quantity.

## Other Signals
- Watch time and CTR remain the strongest ranking factors.
- Use end screens and cards to increase session time.
- Thumbnails have outsized impact on CTR.
